# Portfolio

The portfolio screen is listing all the tokens that you own. There are also a search, filter and sort features.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-03-12 at 18.28.44.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



The portofolio also shows the value of youtr tokens, your total historical profits, the total performance payouts you have received, and the cash you have got available.

From the portfolio you can zoom in on a specific player or open the trade dialog.
