# Community Rewards

SVM  community members are rewarded for their loyalty and commitment and for every action  they perform.

## Type of Actions

* Onboarding: signing up, following on social media, joining Telegram and Discord
* Social engagement
* Using the apps and being an active trader
* sharing ideas and providing feedback
* voting
* finding bugs



## Reward Points

The community rewards are give in reward points. Those points can be:

* exchanged for token packs in SVM Pro (you get real player token s)
* used to increase your loyalty level
* used to participate in a future SVM token airdrop
* user win different prizes<br>
