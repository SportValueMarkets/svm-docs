# Referral Program

If you invite a friend in Free and Pro apps, you will get 1000 points for each referral and your friend will 1000 points when he joins. These points can be used to purchase tokens.\
To obtain the referral link or code, go to:

* [https://free.sportvalue.app/rewards](https://free.sportvalue.app/rewards) for SVM Free
* [https://pro.sportvalue.app/rewards](https://pro.sportvalue.app/rewards) for SVM PRO
